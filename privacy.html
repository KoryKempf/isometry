Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 14, 2026

Isometry ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website playisometry.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We built Isometry to be a clean, ad-free daily puzzle game, and we take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Collect

Guest players (no account required)

If you play without creating an account, we store your puzzle progress, solve history, and streak count in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Registered users (optional account)

If you choose to create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and send password reset emails if needed
  • Display name — optional, chosen by you, shown on leaderboards (defaults to "Anonymous" if not set)
  • Puzzle results — your solve history including date, difficulty, move count, and completion time
  • Streak data — your consecutive daily play streak
  • Account creation date

Google Sign-In (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information from Google. We do not receive access to your Google contacts, Drive, Calendar, or any other Google service. The information received is limited to what is necessary to create and authenticate your Isometry account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To save your puzzle solve history and streak across devices
  • To display your display name on leaderboards (if you have set one)
  • To send password reset emails when requested
  • To improve the game (aggregate, anonymized usage data only)

We do not use your information for advertising, we do not sell your data to any third party, and we do not share your personal information with any third party except as described in this policy.

3. Data Storage and Security

Registered user data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database platform. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth, which follows industry-standard security practices.

Guest data is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage (not tracking cookies) to store your game state, theme preference, and session token if you are signed in. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in. Google Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — website hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access — you can view your puzzle history in the game's archive calendar
  • Deletion — you can delete your account and all associated data by emailing us at the address below. Guest data can be cleared by clearing your browser's localStorage.
  • Portability — contact us to request a copy of your data
  • Display name — you can change or remove your display name at any time in your account settings

7. Children's Privacy

Isometry is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
