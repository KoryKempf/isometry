How to Play
Your daily workout for spatial intelligence.
👁
Read the three views
Each panel shows the hidden structure from a different angle: top, front, and side. Think of them like X-rays of the same object taken from three directions.
🧱
Build it cube by cube
Click cells in the editor to place or remove cubes. Use the layer tabs (Bottom, Middle, Top) to work on each level of the structure.
✅
Match all three views
Your structure must cast the same shadow as each clue view. The live view shows green for matches, red for extra cubes, and yellow outlines for missing ones.
🎯
Par and scoring
Par is the cube count of the reference solution. Multiple valid answers exist, so use fewer cubes to beat par. Hints cost 5 moves each.
📅
A new puzzle every day
Four difficulties drop each day at midnight Eastern. Use the Prev/Next buttons to revisit older puzzles, or browse your archive history.
